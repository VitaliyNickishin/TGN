Our cases

How to bring new customers and make your currency skyrocket through trading competition

As there are many projects, the niche tends to be extremely competitive. In that regard, the key thing is to attract customers to the project with what they do best: being active participants.

So, customers can be rewarded for being active and joining a vibrant community of like-minded customers.

To achieve that, we launched a contest with a $1 mln prize, Bentley, and Bentayga (as a mega grand finale prize). Customers had to buy assets of the project on Binance DEX and keep these funds. As a result, this competition led to 1500 new users, assets worth $1 mln were sold, and the price of it soared.

Creating a strong media strategy is really important for every project, and streaming services are not an exception. Our fruitful collaboration led to strong brand awareness, bringing 16 000 new customers and a group of 1 800 returning clients on the platform.
